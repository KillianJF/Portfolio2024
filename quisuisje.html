HOME WHO AM I ?

Hi, I'm Killian,

I'm a French filmmaker currently traveling around Asia.

Capturing moments of life, memories, and telling stories has been part of my daily life since I was a child. What made me fall in love with the art of video is the joy ofconveying emotions .

I'm also convinced that this is the key to a successful video : the viewer needs to feel your vibe to stay engaged and impacted. That's why "emotion" is the keyword in each of my productions.

I've had the opportunity to work with brands such as Hermès France, Club Med, and Couleur for the Rugby World Cup , and I'm very proud of it!

I would be delighted to keep this momentum going with you! :)
